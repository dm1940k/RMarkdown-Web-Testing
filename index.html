I am a self taught programmer and data analyst in search of my first developer or data analysis position. I have chosen to focus on JS + HTML + CSS for web, C# for software, and R for data. I am also familiar with SQL, Excel, and Ruby. I am currently working on a web based D&D character generator. The most compelling aspect of programming and data analysis for me are the moments when I discover a new way to do something or solve a problem.


I have spent the last 6 years in the liquor industry. As cocktail room manager I found myself using data in order to direct the menu and events programming. In my spirits production role I learned to use Excel for recipe development, which opened my eyes to the possibilities programming offered. I've since used both C# and Javascript to write a volume calculator for our milk-tank-turned-mixing-tank, which quickly converts milk pounds to gallons and liters and is used almost daily.


I hope to grow my knowledge in the skills above and maybe turn them towards my other interests, like mechanical design or carpentry. I plan to also use this site to highlight my endeavours in photography, 3d modeling, and other personal passions.
