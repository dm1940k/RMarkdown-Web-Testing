Dan Moriarty



I am a self taught programmer and data analyst in search of my first developer or data analysis position. I have chosen to focus on C# and ASP.NET for software, and R for data. I am also familiar with HTML, CSS, Excel, SQL, and Ruby. I am currently working to create a D&D dungeon generator, as well as this portfolio site. The most compelling aspect of programming and data analysis for me are the moments when I discover a new way to do something or solve a problem.


I have spent the last 6 years in liquor production and sales. As a cocktail room manager I found myself using data in order to direct our menu and events program. In my production role I learned to use Excel for recipe development, which opened my eyes to the possibilities programming offered. I've since used C# to write a volume calculator for our milk-tank-turned-mixing-tank, which quickly converts milk pounds to gallons and liters and is used almost every week.


I hope to continue to grow my depth and breath of knowledge in the skills above, and maybe even turn them towards my other interests, like mechanical design or carpentry. I plan to use this portfolio site to highlight those endeavours as well.
